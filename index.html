Financial Literacy

Money lessons built for orientation week.

5 lessons. 2 minutes each. Built into the app for first-year students.

💡
The Envelope Method
Divide your money into labelled categories. When a category is empty, stop spending there. Simple and powerful.
Lesson 1
📉
The 50/30/20 Rule
50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings. Even saving 5–10% is a great start. P100/month = P1,200/year.
Lesson 2
🧠
Spending Triggers
Boredom, stress, peer pressure cause most overspending. Identify your triggers — that's 80% of the solution.
Lesson 3
🎯
Why Goals Work
Vague saving never works. A specific named goal — "Freedom Fund: P3,000" — gives your brain a commitment to honour.
Lesson 4
📊
Proactive Budgeting
Check what you have LEFT before going to the mall, not after. That one habit change makes everything else easier.
